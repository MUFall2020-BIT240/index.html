Our Homemade Dishes



The Famous Cheesy Alfredo The Phenomenal Baked Ziti The Classic Spaghetti & Meatballs
The Cheesy Alfredo is our most famous dish dating back to the ancestors of the business. Passed down between each generation the same consistent recipe that has yet to be changed will continuously be used as each year it grows more favorited by the population. The Baked Ziti is composed of a batch of freshly cooked ziti, oven-baked with homemade marinara sauce topped with freshly rolled meatballs. It is a dish that takes long hours of work to prepare prior to serving, requiring the chefs to begin the meal prepare a day in advance for the following business day in order for the dish to come out perfect for the customers. The Classic Spaghetti & Meatballs is composed of fresh spaghetti topped with hand-rolled meatballs, it is always an easy choice if the customer wants something very simple that they have had prior to showing up to the restaurant.
